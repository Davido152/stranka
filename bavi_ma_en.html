Probably the most important part of my life. Without it, I don't know where I'd be now. Started about seven years ago, and enjoy it more and more by the day, with a strong desire to keep progressing.
List of instruments I play:

  • Drums
  • Bass
  • A little guitar
  • Some piano
  • Nerves
  • Triangle (maybe the hardest of all mentioned)
  • Cajón
  • Table/My legs/Well, anything that can be hit (falls under the drums cathegory)

I've been playing drums the longest, for about seven years now (I don't keep exact track). It's my main instrument. Two years after starting drums, I began learning bass, and even though I don't prioritize it as much, I sometimes play it even more. I'm actually in two bands as a bassist. I've learned some guitar and piano because i sometimes play then for fun whenever they're just lying around. As for the other instruments, I learned them because of music school (where percussionists are often needed).
